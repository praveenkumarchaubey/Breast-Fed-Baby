About this Initiative

Breast-Feeding is prerequisite for infants. Indian government do not have a proper data collection to know whether mothers are initiating breast-feeds in first hour of life after birth and continuing it for at least six months. It is an Indian Government initiative in collaboration with Institute Of Informatics and Communication (I.I.C.), Delhi University. The purpose of this initiative is to help government collect the data by building up a software based module, so that doctor, nurse, mother or relative will give information about breastfeeding and introduction of solid foods. The data thus collected will be further analysed for providing better health facilities in India.
